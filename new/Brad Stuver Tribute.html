STUUUUUUU!

A tribute to Brad Stuver, Austin FC's 2021 Defensive Player of the Year

Entering MLS

After his senior season of college, Stuver was invited to the 2013 MLS Combine. He was the only Horizon League player selected to the combine, and was one of seven collegiate goalkeepers to earn a spot. On January 17, 2013 Stuver was drafted in the second round (32nd overall) of the 2013 MLS SuperDraft by Montreal Impact. He was the first goalkeeper chosen in the draft. The Impacted declined to sign Stuver, so he was signed by the MLS as a pool goalkeeper for the 2013 season.

On December 1, 2017, Crew SC declined Stuver's contract option, ending his four-year tenure with the club. During his time in Columbus, Stuver made seven appearances for Crew SC in official competition. He appeared just twice in league play.

Becoming Verde

Acquisition

On December 28, 2020, Stuver was acquired by Austin FC as a free agent.

Inaugural Season Stats

Brad quickly made a name for himself with some remarkable saves. Here is a quick snapshot of King Brad's stats in his inaugural season as the Verde keeper:

  • Apps: 33
  • Minutes Played: 2970
  • Saves: 136
  • Goals Conceded: 53
  • Clean Sheets: 5

Community Engagement

Beyond being a leader on the pitch, Brad also gives back to the Austin community by organizing the "Laundry Project", an effort that helps Austin families by covering expenses at laundromats around the city. Brad was recently recognized by the MLS as one of the finalists for Humanitarian of the year.
